About The Event

MAHOTSAV - THE ANNUAL ART FESTIVAL OF BIET JHANSI IS ONE OF THE LARGEST AND MOST ANTICIPATED FESTIVALS THROUGHOUT THE REGION. CONTINUING ITS RICH LEGACY OF 12 YEARS, MAHOTSAV COMBINES THE ZEAL AND DESIRE OF THE STUDENTS TO SHOWCASE AND CELEBRATE THEIR AMAZING ART TALENT. IT HAS BEEN THE TOUCHSTONE OF INDIAN AS WELL AS FOREIGN ART HERITAGE.

Where

Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering and Technology, Jhansi, Uttar Pradesh - 284128

When

Saturday to Wednesday
1st to 5th April, 2023
