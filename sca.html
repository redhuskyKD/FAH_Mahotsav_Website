Scandal

Create scandalous & hilariousnews headlines and full-fill your fantasy of becoming an editor for a day !!! Come and enjoy one of the funniest event!!!

Rules for the event

Relevant material will be provided to you.

It is a solo event.

No restriction on headlines.

No external help will be provided .

Time limit of 1 hr.

Judging criteria :- work, discipline, cleanliness, interpretation.

Team with any indiscipline activities will be disqualified.

Mahotsav is the annual event organised by Fine Arts & Hobbies Sub-Council of Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering and Technology, Jhansi. Many artistic, craftic and fun events are held for the students to enjoy. It is one of those flagship college events where the students from all years of Engineering come to enjoy and make memories.
